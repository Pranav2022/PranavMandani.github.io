Obstructive Sleep Apnea (OSA)

Obstructive Sleep Apnea (OSA), a prevalent sleep disorder, is characterized by repeated interruptions in breathing during sleep, leading to momentary awakenings. In March 2022, I played a pivotal role in a 4-person team, contributing to the design and implementation of an OSA application. Leveraging Python, Flask, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, along with machine learning using Random Forest with 95% accuracy and a 30% recall rate, the application empowers users to monitor sleep patterns and identify potential indicators of OSA. Additionally, in a separate initiative with a 3-person team, we successfully developed a SmartCare System, employing similar technologies and machine learning methodologies to analyze sleep patterns and detect signs of OSA, further enhancing our commitment to sleep health.

Intelligent Latex equation Sequencer using vector mask overlay and Localised Composition analysis

Led a team of 4 selected students, out of 250 interviewed, for an innovative project at TextMover Labs LLC—developing an Intelligent Latex Equation Sequencer. This educational application employs interactive animations to teach mathematical derivations virtually. The system comprises three modules: the first converts Latex equations to SVG images, the second breaks down SVG images using a CNN for element recognition, and the third animates each recognized element. Achieved a 95% accuracy rate with a CNN algorithm for handwritten character identification, and seamlessly integrated advanced animation techniques using Docker, ReactJs, nodeJs, and CNN for an enhanced visual presentation of mathematical derivations.

Krishi - An interface for Farmers

Krishi is a comprehensive web application designed to facilitate the agricultural community by providing a seamless online platform for buying and selling agricultural products. Developed using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and MySQL, Krishi offers a user-friendly interface for farmers to sign up, log in, and create detailed product listings, complete with photos, pricing, and discounts. Farmers can enhance their engagement through personalized profiles, while also gaining valuable insights into farming techniques and cutting-edge technologies through the integrated blog feature. This platform empowers users to share their expertise by contributing blogs and comments, fostering a collaborative community where farmers can find solutions to agricultural challenges. Additionally, consumers can easily sign up, browse products, and make secure online purchases, contributing to the growth and efficiency of the agricultural ecosystem.
